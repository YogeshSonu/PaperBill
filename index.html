Frequently Asked Questions

Is Vyapar really free to use? +

Yes, Vyapar offers a completely free version with all essential features for small retailers including unlimited invoicing, basic inventory management, and expense tracking. There's also a premium version with additional capabilities like multi-user access, advanced reporting, and priority support for growing businesses.

Can I use Vyapar without internet? +

Absolutely! Vyapar works completely offline, and your data syncs automatically when you're back online. This makes it perfect for areas with unreliable internet connections. All features including invoicing, inventory management, and reporting are available offline.

How does Vyapar help with GST compliance? +

Vyapar automatically generates GST-compliant invoices with all required fields including HSN/SAC codes, GSTIN, and tax breakdowns. It prepares GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B reports that can be directly uploaded to the GST portal. The software also tracks input tax credit and calculates your tax liability automatically.

Can I access my data on multiple devices? +

Yes, Vyapar allows you to access your business data across multiple devices. Your data syncs securely between your phone, tablet, and computer. With premium plans, you can have multiple users accessing the same business data simultaneously from different devices.

How is my data protected? +

Vyapar uses bank-grade 256-bit encryption for all data transmission and storage. Your data is automatically backed up daily and can be exported anytime. We never share your data with third parties without your consent. You can also enable two-factor authentication for added security.
